# Algorithms Book

For a few weeks, I worked on implementing the major algorithms in the 4<sup>th</sup> edition of Algorithms by  Robert Sedgewick and Kevin Wayne. The reason behind this decision is quite simple, it'd get my journey on learning Rust truly started.

> I must also truly acknowledge R. Sedgewick and K. Wayne for such an amazing book, it absolutely compiles a variety of different algorithms and their use-cases in one book. It even touches on some ideas used in Quantum Computing.

I was introduced to this book through my lectures at the IT University of Copenhagen. Since I was already interested in learning and developing Rust skills, I decided to take this as a small project. I've started with most of the big algorithms as laid out in the book. And what happens next is still undecided, so let's find out?
